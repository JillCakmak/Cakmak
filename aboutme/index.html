The Brain behind Typologically

Ever wondered who is behind the scenes?

I am a Media, Journalism and Publishing student at the Oxford Brookes University. I was born and raised in Germany by my lovely parents, grew up with my three siblings as well as our dog and cat.(Yes you can be a dog AND a cat person). It was not until 2020 that I came to the UK to start my future career.

What I did & what I want to do

Before I started my course in the UK I studied food chemistry in Germany for three years. To be honest with you, that career was not creative and flexible enough for me. But I still share the love of scientific adventures and logic behind little things. So Typologically was born, the rest is history ;)

Futuristically speaking, I would like to combine design and creativity with the knowledge and theories I gained in the past. Design has many connections to scientific precision, you could argue that I didn't completly change my direction. But this is much more fun.

What I love

I am happy about every nicely brewed cup of coffee, especially when its accompanied by a wonderful novel.
